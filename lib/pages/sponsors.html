The Medical Defence Union is the largest medical defence organisation in the United Kingdom. It provides doctors, dentists, medical students and other healthcare professionals with medicolegal representation and advice across the UK. The MDU offers free student membership entitling members to: a free medical dictionary; access to the MDU's library of medicolegal articles and discounts on core medical textbooks.

The MDU also runs The Electives Network, a comprehensive service offering unique connections with leading hospitals across the world, monthly cash prizes for elective photos and diaries as well as ethical and legal advice during your elective planning.

The Institute of Medical Ethics is a charitable organisation which aims to improve the education and debate of medical ethics. The IME founded and co-own the Journal of Medical Ethics under the BMJ and publish world-leading ethical reserach and debate. Their mission is to promote and support the study and understanding of medical ethics in clinical practice through education, research and publication.

The IME are also a leading organisation in the funding of student-driven ethical enquiry and provision of grants for students interested in ethical aspects of medicine. They also financially support organisations, such as ourselves, who aim to improve the provision of ethics teaching in medical school curricula.
